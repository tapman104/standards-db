# Standard Overview

ISO 7090 specifies plain washers (chamfered bore) in normal series, product grade A. Hardness 300HV. The chamfer on the inner bore accommodates the chamfer at the root of hex bolt heads (ISO 4014/4017), ensuring full bearing face contact. Used with class 8.8+ bolts.

The 300HV hardness prevents embedding of the washer face into softer workpiece materials. GOST 11371-78 covers both 200HV (no chamfer) and 300HV (chamfer) forms. DIN 125-B is the withdrawn predecessor.
