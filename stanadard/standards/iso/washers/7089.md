# Standard Overview

ISO 7089 specifies plain washers (no chamfer) in normal series, product grade A (precision). Hardness 200HV. Used under bolt heads and nuts to distribute clamping load, prevent surface damage, and bridge slot/hole clearances.

Direct GOST equivalent: GOST 11371-78 (200HV form). German: DIN 125-A. Indian: IS 2016. See also ISO 7090 (same washer with chamfered bore).
