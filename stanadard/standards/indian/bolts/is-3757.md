# Standard Overview

IS 3757 specifies high-strength structural bolts for use in friction-grip and bearing-type structural steel connections in Indian civil and structural engineering. Uses property class designation with suffix S (structural): 8.8S and 10.9S. Single-use requirement (proof load tightening = permanent set).

Equivalent to ISO 7411 and GOST 22353-77 in application. Part of the Indian structural bolt assembly with IS 6623 (structural nuts) and IS 6649 (structural washers).
