# Standard Overview

IS 5624 specifies foundation bolts for anchoring machinery, structural frames, and equipment to concrete or masonry foundations. Covers multiple types (straight, L-shaped/J-shaped, plate type). Equivalent to GOST 24379.0-80 / GOST 24379.1-80 in scope. DIN 529 is the German reference.

Used extensively in Indian industrial construction — pumps, motors, compressors, turbines, and structural columns.

# Materials

Low-carbon steel for types 1–2; medium-carbon for higher-stress applications. Hot-dip galvanized or epoxy coated for corrosive environments.
