# Standard Overview

DIN 933 was the German standard for fully threaded hexagon head screws. **Withdrawn** and replaced by ISO 4017. Dimensions are identical to ISO 4017; there are no WAF differences of significance at the common sizes. A safe 1:1 substitution for new orders.

# Withdrawal Note

DIN 933 is **withdrawn**. New designs must specify ISO 4017. Substitution is safe — no dimensional differences at common sizes.
