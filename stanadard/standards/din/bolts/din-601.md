# Standard Overview

DIN 601 was the German standard for grade C (black/rough tolerance) hexagon head bolts. **Withdrawn** and replaced by ISO 4016. Used in structural steel, civil construction, and agricultural machinery where high precision is not required. Property class limited to 5.8 maximum.
