# Standard Overview

IS 1364 Part 1 specifies hexagon head bolts with partial thread shank (equivalent to ISO 4014). Published by Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS). Harmonised with ISO 4014 for dimensional compatibility with internationally sourced fasteners. Product grades A and B.

Used in Indian engineering projects, infrastructure, and manufacturing as the primary hex bolt specification.
