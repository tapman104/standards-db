# Standard Overview

IS 7002 specifies prevailing torque hexagon lock nuts with nylon (polyamide) insert for metric threads. **Unlike ISO (which separates ISO 7040 low form and ISO 7041 high form into two standards), IS 7002 covers both forms in a single document.** Form must be specified explicitly when ordering.

Temperature limit: 120°C continuous (PA6/PA66 insert). Maximum 5 reuse cycles. HDG prohibited.

# Important Ordering Note

IS 7002 covers both forms. **Always specify "Low Form" or "High Form" explicitly.** Failure to specify form may result in supply of either form.
