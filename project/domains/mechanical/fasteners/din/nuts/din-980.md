# Standard Overview

DIN 980 was the German standard for all-metal prevailing torque lock nuts (Stover/oval nut). **Withdrawn** and replaced by ISO 7042. Dimensions identical. No polymer insert — suitable for high-temperature applications up to 300°C+. HDG compatible (unlike DIN 985/DIN 982 nyloc nuts).

Two forms: Form V (standard) and Form A (reduced height). ISO 7042 consolidates both.
