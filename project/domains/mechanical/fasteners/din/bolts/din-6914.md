# Standard Overview

DIN 6914 was the German standard for high-strength structural bolts with large WAF, used in friction-grip structural steel connections. **Withdrawn** and superseded by EN 14399-4 (part of the European structural bolting assembly series EN 14399). Property class 10.9 only.

Was part of the DIN 6914/6915/6916 assembly system (bolt/nut/washer). These three standards have been collectively replaced by EN 14399.

# Assembly System

DIN 6914 was designed to be used as part of the DIN structural bolt assembly:
- DIN 6914 (bolt) → replaced by EN 14399-4
- DIN 6915 (nut) → replaced by EN 14399-6
- DIN 6916 (washer) → replaced by EN 14399-6

Replace the entire assembly set together when modernising.

# Withdrawal Note

DIN 6914 is **withdrawn**. New structural designs must use EN 14399-4. Existing legacy structures may retain original DIN 6914 components; replacement orders now use EN 14399-4.
