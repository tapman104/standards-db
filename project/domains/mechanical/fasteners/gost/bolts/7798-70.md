# Standard Overview

GOST 7798-70 specifies hexagon head bolts with a partially threaded shank, produced to accuracy class B. The bolt features a hexagonal head and a cylindrical unthreaded shank with thread covering only part of the bolt length, making it suitable for joints where the bolt passes through clearance holes and clamps two or more parts together. The unthreaded shank section bears the shear load across the joint interface; the threaded portion provides clamping force through the nut. Thread diameters range from M6 to M48 with lengths up to 300 mm.

Widely used in mechanical engineering, structural steel fabrication, pipeline flanges, pump and compressor mounts, and general equipment installation. The standard corresponds closely to ISO 4014 and German DIN 931. Minor dimensional differences exist at M10, M12, M14, and M22 in the width across flats (WAF). Indian equivalent is IS 1364 Part 1.

# Surface Treatments

| GOST Code | Treatment | ISO Equivalent | Min Thickness | Corrosion Resistance |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|---------------|----------------------|
| 00 | Bare / black oxide | — | — | Protected/indoor only |
| 01 | Zinc electroplating, yellow chromate | ISO 4042 Zn.6 ye | 6 µm | ~96 h salt spray |
| 03 | Zinc electroplating, clear chromate | ISO 4042 Zn.9 cl | 9 µm | ~144 h salt spray |
| 13 | Hot-dip galvanizing (HDG) | ISO 10684 | 45–85 µm | 1000+ h salt spray |
| 40 | Phosphate + oil | GOST 9.301 Fos/м | ~5 µm | Mild indoor protection |

**⚠ Hydrogen Embrittlement (Class 10.9):** Mandatory de-embrittlement bake at 190–210°C for ≥4 h within 4 h of acid-bath electroplating completion. For class 10.9, prefer mechanical zinc plating or zinc-flake (Geomet/Dacromet) to eliminate hydrogen risk entirely.

**HDG thread tolerance:** Bolts for HDG are manufactured with 6az thread tolerance (undersized) to remain 6g after zinc build-up. HDG nuts must be tapped oversize post-coating. Order bolt-and-nut sets matched.

# Marking Requirements

Marking location: head top face or hexagon side flat.

- Property class (e.g., "4.6", "8.8", "10.9")
- Manufacturer identification code — mandatory for class 8.8 and above
- Left-hand thread: marked "LH"; right-hand thread is unmarked
- Coating code is in the designation string only — not marked on the fastener

Per GOST 1759.1-82 and ISO 898-1 Clause 11.
