# Standard Overview

ISO 4014 specifies hexagon head bolts with a partially threaded shank in product grades A (precision) and B (general). It is the primary international reference for this fastener type, superseding legacy national standards including DIN 931 (withdrawn). The unthreaded shank section bears shear loads at the joint interface; the threaded section provides clamping force via the nut.

Direct GOST equivalents: GOST 7817-80 (grade A, up to M24) and GOST 7798-70 (grade B, M6–M48). The Indian equivalent IS 1364-1 is harmonised with this standard.
