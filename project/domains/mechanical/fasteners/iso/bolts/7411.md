# Standard Overview

ISO 7411 specifies high-strength structural bolts with large WAF (width across flats), designed for friction-grip and bearing-type structural steel connections. The enlarged WAF allows higher tightening torque application and wider washer bearing area than standard ISO 4014 bolts.

Direct GOST equivalents: GOST 22353-77 (Soviet-era) and GOST R 52644-2006 (modern RF). European equivalent: EN 14399-4 (part of the EN 14399 structural bolt assembly series). Indian equivalent: IS 3757.

# Mechanical Properties

Property class 10.9 minimum. Class 12.9 in some national variants. Single-use (proof load tightening = permanent set). See standards/gost/bolts/22353-77.md for full detail.

# Installation

Friction-grip joints: controlled tightening to proof load using calibrated torque wrench, turn-of-nut method, or direct tension indicator. Single-use: do not reuse once removed. See standards/gost/bolts/22353-77.md for torque values.
