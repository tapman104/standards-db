# Standard Overview

ISO 4017 specifies hexagon head screws that are fully threaded from tip to immediately below the head. They are used when threading directly into tapped holes or when maximum thread engagement is required. Product grades A (M1.6–M24) and B (M24+) apply.

Direct GOST equivalent: GOST 7805-70. Indian equivalent: IS 1364-2. DIN 933 is the withdrawn German predecessor (dimensions identical to ISO 4017).
