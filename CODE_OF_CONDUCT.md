# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional community for engineers, contributors, and maintainers.

## Expected Behavior

Contributors are expected to:
- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on technical facts and evidence
- Accept feedback professionally
- Credit sources and prior work appropriately

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Deliberate misinformation in technical submissions
- Repeatedly ignoring project contribution rules

## Standards Discussions

Disagreements about technical details are normal. Resolve them by:
- Citing official or authoritative references
- Comparing revision years and applicability
- Documenting uncertainty in the data fields

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take appropriate action, including warning, rejecting contributions, or restricting participation for repeated violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe inappropriate behavior, report it to the maintainers through the project issue tracker with relevant details.

## Attribution

This policy is inspired by common open-source community standards and adapted for this engineering data project.
